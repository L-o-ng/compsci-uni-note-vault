$$c^2=a^2+b^2$$
## Proof
Consider this triangle:
![[Pythagoras Theorem Proof.png]]
We have 3 [[Similar Triangles|similar triangles]].
$\text{Area }A=\lambda a^2,\space\text{Area }B=\lambda b^2,\space\text{Area }C=\lambda c^2$
Area A + Area B = Area C
$\lambda a^2+\lambda b^2 = \lambda c^2$
$\implies a^2+b^2=c^2$

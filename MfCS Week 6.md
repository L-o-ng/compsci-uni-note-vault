#coursework 

1) Write matrices for the following linear transformations:
	1) Scaling by a factor of 3: $$\begin{bmatrix}
3 & 0 \\
0 & 3
\end{bmatrix}$$
	2) Reflection across $y=x$: $$\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}$$
	3) Rotation clockwise by $90^\circ$: $$\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1  \\
-1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}$$
2) $$\array{\text{Scale then Reflect: }\begin{bmatrix}
3 & 0 \\
0 & 3
\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}
3 & 0 \\
0 & 3
\end{bmatrix} \text{, commutative}\\ 
\text{Reflect then Rotate: }\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
-1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
-1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}\text{, commutative}\\ 
\text{Scale then Rotate: }\begin{bmatrix}
3 & 0 \\
0 & 3
\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
-1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}
-3 & 0 \\
0 & 3
\end{bmatrix}\text{, not commutative}}$$

There are 2 types of question.
## Definite Integrals
Compute $$\int_{a}^b f(x)dx$$
1) Find an [[Anti-Derivative]] $F(x)\text{ such that }F^\prime(x)=f(x)$
2) Compute $F(b)=F(a)$
## Indefinite Integral
Compute $$\int f(x)dx$$
1) Find an [[Anti-Derivative]] $F(x)\text{ such that }F^\prime(x)=f(x)$
2) Write $\int f(x)dx=F(x)+c$

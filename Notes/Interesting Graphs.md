## The Acquaintance Graph
In the **acquaintance graph**, the vertices are all people, and there is an edge between two of them if they are acquainted.

Many sociologists observed that this graph has diameter 6 after removing a small fraction of the vertices. This is known as the small world phenomenon.
## The Collaboration Graph
In the **collaboration graph**, the vertices are all people (or only scientists), and there is an edge between two of them if they have written a joint paper.

Paul Erdos was a prolific mathematician. The Erdos Number of a person is the distance to Paul Erdos in the collaboration graph.
## The Hollywood Graph
In the **Hollywood graph**, the vertices are all people (or only actors), and there is an edge between two of them if they have appeared in the same film.

Kevin Bacon is a famous Hollywood actor. The Bacon number of a person is the distance to Kevin Bacon in the Hollywood graph.
## The Erdos-Bacon Number
>For any person, their Erdos-Bacon number is the sum of their Erdos Number and their Bacon Number.

There are not many people with a small Erdos-Bacon number.

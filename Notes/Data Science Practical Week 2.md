		1. $10\times 10\times 10\times 10=10000$

		1. $10\times 9\times 8\times 7=5040$

		1. $10\times 9\times 8\times 1 =720$
6. In how many ways can 7 people be seated at a round table (rotations do not change the arrangement) if:

		1. $\frac{7!}{7}=720$

		1. $720 - (6-1)!\times 2=480$

#topic 

Consider the infinite sum:
$$
\frac{1}{1^{4}}+\frac{1}{2^{4}}+\frac{1}{3^{4}}+\cdots+\frac{1}{n^{4}}+\frac{1}{(n+1)^{4}}+\cdots
$$
We have:
$$
\frac{1}{1^{4}}+\frac{1}{2^{4}}+\frac{1}{3^{4}}+\frac{1}{4^{4}}+\frac{1}{5^{5}}\approx 1.08035\dots
$$
and 
$$
\frac{1}{1^{4}}+\frac{1}{2^{4}}+\frac{1}{3^{4}}+\frac{1}{4^{4}}+\frac{1}{5^{5}}+\cdots \frac{1}{10^{4}}\approx 1.0820365\dots
$$
This may tend to an individual limit.

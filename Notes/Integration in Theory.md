## Formalities
### Definition 1
The integral $\int_{a}^b f(x)dx$ measures the area between the graph of $f(x)$ and the $x\text{-axis}$, between $a$ and $b$.

### Definition 2
Let $a,b \in \mathbb{R}$, $f(x)$ a real continuous function between $a$ and $b$.
Let $N \in \mathbb{N}, N>0$ and $w=\frac{b-a}{N}$.
We define $$\int^{b}_{a}f(x)dx=\lim_{ N \to \infty } \sum_{k=1}^Nwf(a+kw)$$Which we call the integral of $f$ from $a$ to $b$.

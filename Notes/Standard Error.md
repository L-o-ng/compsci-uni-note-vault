#definition 

The **standard error** of a sample statistic is the standard deviation of its sampling distribution.

| Statistic                                                        | Standard Error                                        |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Mean $\bar{X}$                                                   | $\sigma_{\bar{X}}=\frac{\mathbf{\sigma}}{\sqrt{ n }}$ |
| Proportion $\hat{p}$, the fraction of sample with same condition | $\sigma_{\hat{p}}=\sqrt{ \frac{p(1-p)}{n} }$          |

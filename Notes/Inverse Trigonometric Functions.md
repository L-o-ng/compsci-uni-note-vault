## $\arcsin$
The inverse of $\sin$ is $\arcsin$.
### Definition
For $-1\le x\le{1},\arcsin x$ is defined as the **unique** **solution** to $\sin(\arcsin x)=x\text{ with }-\frac{\pi}{2}\le \arcsin x\le \frac{\pi}{2}$

## $\arccos$
The inverse of $\cos$ is $\arccos$.
### Definition
For $-1\le x\le{1},\arccos x$ is defined as the **unique** **solution** to $\cos(\arccos x)=x\text{ with }-0\le \arccos x\le\pi$

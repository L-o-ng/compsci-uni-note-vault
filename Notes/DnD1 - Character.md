## Orsik Torevir - Mountain Dwarf Barbarian 
[Character Sheet](https://www.dndbeyond.com/characters/141432822)
#### Appearance 
Orsik is a blue-eyed, white-haired mountain dwarf. He has blue eyes; is fair-skinned with rugged, rounded features; and his bald head is complemented by a great beard, braided and clasped with silver shackles. 
#### Backstory 
Orsik spent his first few decades training in combat and smithing deep within {name} Keep. He later took up the post of guarding the keep entrance, where he became familiar with Gundren Rockseeker, who dealt with the keep on regular occasions. One day, from the heart of the keep, a creature emerged from within the mountain and decimated its lofty halls, leaving Orsik as the sole survivor at the gate, fleeing, stricken with terror and guilt. He eventually find his way into the home and hearth of Gundren, who helps him find his place in this new life, but Orsik dreams of the strength to reclaim the treasure and pride deep within the mountain. 
#### Traits 
+ Growing up in secluded mountains made many new interactions difficult for Orsik. He prefers to deal quickly, refusing to haggle and making an attempt to never abandon a job. 
+ Orsik thrives in the pursuit of power. Be it wealth or weaponry, he will never refuse a step on his path to reclaim his home.
